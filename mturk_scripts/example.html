There are 59 rows (pairs of videos) in this HIT. For each row, click "Show Videos" to view the videos in that row.

Before each video appears, a dot will flash twice to indicate where the video will play.

After viewing both videos, choose which of the two videos appears more realistic to you: left or right. Your submission may be rejected if you make a mistake in an obvious case. Your submission may be rejected if you submit an incomplete HIT.

The first 3 rows are for warm-up. They can also be viewed during HIT Preview.
